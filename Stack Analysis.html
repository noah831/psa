Stack Consolidation Analysis

Evaluating PSA and Resource Management tools to replace Everhour and unify Salesforce, QuickBooks, and Jira.

The Current Ecosystem & The Challenge

Before selecting a new tool, it is critical to understand the current technological footprint. Your organization currently relies on a fragmented stack. The primary challenge is finding a central Professional Services Automation (PSA) or Resource Management tool that can absorb the time-tracking functions of Everhour while seamlessly bridging the gap between your CRM, your financial software, and your development tracking tool without introducing double data entry.

The Market Landscape

The tools evaluated fall into three distinct categories. Understanding this categorization is essential because it dictates how heavy the implementation will be and what primary problem the tool attempts to solve. Lightweight tools focus purely on who is doing what, while heavy enterprise tools attempt to run the entire business lifecycle.

Resource Management (Float, Runn)

Highly visual tools prioritizing scheduling and capacity. They integrate well with Jira but have lighter financial syncing with QuickBooks.

Modern PSA (Ruddr, Rocketlane, Forecast)

The middle ground. They offer solid resource planning combined with strong time tracking and direct financial integrations. Forecast adds AI-driven Jira syncing.

Enterprise / Heavy (Accelo, ERPNext)

Massive, comprehensive platforms designed to be your single source of truth. Highly capable but require immense setup time and process changes.

🎯 The Sweet Spot

Ruddr and Forecast.app provide the most balanced shapes, offering robust integrations while maintaining a user interface that won't overwhelm a smaller team migrating off Everhour.

Strategic Recommendations

Based on the data visualized above, the decision rests on your organization's primary pain point. If development alignment is critical, go Jira-first. If financial consolidation is key, adopt a Modern PSA. Below are the tailored recommendations based on specific business postures.

The Balanced Approach

Recommend: Ruddr
  • ✔️ Easily replaces Everhour
  • ✔️ Strong QuickBooks integration
  • ✔️ Right-sized pricing for SMBs
  • ❌ Jira sync is good, but not bi-directional

The Jira-First Approach

Recommend: Forecast.app
  • ✔️ Best-in-class Jira bi-directional sync

The Enterprise Shift

Recommend: Accelo
  • ✔️ Replaces Everhour, potentially overlaps Salesforce
  • ✔️ Ultimate deep integration with QB/SF
